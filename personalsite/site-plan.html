Overview

Purpose

I want to make a website where I can post pictures of nature and animals. Basically, a picture blog. There would be pages separating landscapes, animals, and other photos. I think it would be cool to have a place to share some photos I’ve taken of some nearby national parks. My unique selling point would be the fact that the pictures are all unique

Audience

Anyone interested in photography or nature. We appeal to lovers of nature, landscapes, or animals!

Content

Home page

This is a blog type site where I can post photos of nature I have taken. I have numerous photos I’ve taken over the years and think this is the perfect place to share them. I would also like to share little stories alongside the photos or context of where it was taken and why I took it. Hopefully anyone visiting the site will enjoy the photos and keep coming back to see any updates posted.

Questions I expect to be asked will hopefully be answered by the little stories attached to each image. Otherwise, I’m not 100% sure what kind of questions may be asked.

Activities are looking at photos, and reading stories.

People will come to my site because it has photos that they wouldn’t see anywhere else. All of the photos I will have taken personally and don’t appear anywhere else.
